    • SPECIALIZED MARTIAL ARTS TRAINING IN CENTRAL SQUARE

    Jiu-jitsu

    Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ) is a ground-based martial art from Brazil, focusing on submissions and control. It's used for self-defense and sport. Practitioners wear a gi and progress through colored belts, with black belt as the highest rank.

    Karate

    Karate is a Japanese martial art centered on striking with hands and feet, emphasizing discipline, self-control, and strength development. Practitioners train in forms and sparring for self-defense, fitness, and personal growth, using uniforms and colored belts to show rank.

    Muay Thai

    Muay Thai is a Thai martial art and sport using fists, elbows, knees, and shins for striking. It's popular for self-defense, fitness, and competition.

    Judo

    Judo is a Japanese martial art focused on throws and ground control, emphasizing leverage and balance. It's both a sport and a self-defense system.

    Weight Training

    Increase your muscle quality and metabolism with our personalized weight training, emphasizing proper form and technique for increased gains and safety.

    Wrestling

    One of the best and oldest forms of personal combat, we offer expert wrestling coaching.
